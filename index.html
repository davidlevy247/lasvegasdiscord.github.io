Amazing Features

Discord is an incredibly powerful and useful app that grew from a modest VOIP for video gamers to now a feature rich general purpose social media platform

  • Clean DesignDiscord has multiple user selectable appearances and has a clean and easy to understand user interface.
  • Easy to NavigateDiscord is very easy to navigate so you can find what you need, when you need it.
  • Many communitiesDiscord is used by millions of people all over the world with many different hobbies and communities.
  • Easy to useThe Discord app is very easy to use, to connect with friends, and to share content.
  • Free to useThe core features of Discord are completely free! There is a subscription addon available called Nitro where you can unlock fun features.
  • Amazing FeaturesDiscord not only allows content wall style chat rooms, friend to friend calls, group messages, and voice chats, but also offers video streaming too!
